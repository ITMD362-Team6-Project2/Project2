About Berkshire Hathaway

Berkshire Hathaway is a multinational conglomerate holding company known for its commitment to excellence, strong leadership, and significant contributions to the investment industry.

Our Mission and Values

At Berkshire Hathaway, our mission is to deliver long-term value to our shareholders and partners through ethical practices, sustainable growth, and innovative solutions. Our core values include integrity, excellence, and accountability.

Our History

1839

Originally founded as Valley Falls Company, a Textile manufacturing company founded in Rhode Island.

1929

Valley Falls Company merges with Berkshire Cotton Manufacturing Company to form Berkshire Fine Spinning Associates.

1955

Berkshire Fine Spinning Associates merges with Hathaway Manufacturing Company, becoming Berkshire Hathaway Inc.

1962

Warren Buffett begins buying Berkshire Hathaway shares as an undervalued investment opportunity.

1965

Warren Buffett takes control of Berkshire Hathaway, transitioning it into a diversified investment powerhouse.

1985

Berkshire Hathaway closes its textile operations to focus entirely on investments and acquisitions.

1996

Berkshire acquires GEICO, a major auto insurance provider, solidifying its position in the insurance industry.

2010

Acquires Burlington Northern Santa Fe (BNSF), one of the largest railroad systems in North America.

Present

Berkshire Hathaway is a leading global conglomerate with subsidiaries in industries ranging from insurance to utilities.
